Ramen Shoedles

"A cheap meal on a shoestring budget."

In this daring take on the classic Ramen noodles dish, we add some flavour that

you simply can't find anywhere else but in an already worn shoe.

Be sure to use a really old shoe, so that you can avoid that new shoe taste.

Ingredients

  • An old shoe (you can find these at just about any dump)
  • Two packets of Ramen Noodles (just steal some from your local shop)
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Open the packet of Ramen and break the contents into the shoe.
  2. Add the water to a kettle and boil.
  3. Pour the water into the shoe.
  4. Wait three minutes to let it really soak in.
  5. Eat that shit right out of the shoe you fucking animal.
