Sunnyside Chicken Sandwich

"It's surprisingly simple to make!"

When Burger King and KFC first tried to make this dish, they made it onto the news.

Now, for the first time, you too can have access to the original Uncle JoJo recipe!

Ingredients

  • Two pieces of bread (easy to find)
  • Raw chicken (get creative!)
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Butter the bread.
  2. Put the chicken between the pieces of bread.
  3. Put the sandwich in the afternoon sun for several hours.
  4. Enjoy!
